## üìÅ Overview
The **HR Salary Analysis Dataset** consists of 650 cleaned employee records containing demographic details, job roles, compensation, experience, and performance metrics. This dataset powers an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to provide insights into workforce distribution, salary patterns, performance, and HR-related KPIs.

## üìä Dashboard Highlights
The dashboard delivers a comprehensive view of HR and compensation analytics through:

### **1. Workforce Demographics**
- Gender distribution with a clear split across Male, Female, and Other categories  
- Employee count across all major cities (Chicago, Dallas, Houston, Los Angeles, Miami)  
- Department-wise headcount overview  

### **2. Employment Structure**
- Employment type breakdown (Full-Time, Part-Time, Contract, Internship) across departments  
- Hierarchical distribution of designations such as Analyst, Associate, Team Lead, Manager, Executive, Intern  

### **3. Salary & Compensation Insights**
- **Average salary:** 89.78K  
- **Total salary expense:** 58.36M  
- **Maximum bonus recorded:** 29.18K  
- **Total compensation value:** 65.53M  
- Average salary by department with IT, Marketing, Finance, and Sales leading  

### **4. Experience & Performance Overview**
- **Average experience:** 11.7 years  
- Performance rating distribution across six categories: Excellent, Good, Average, Below Average, Poor  
- Department-wise performance matrix showing averages across ratings  

### **5. Interactive Filters**
- Location filter  
- Department filter  
- Experience slider for dynamic analysis
